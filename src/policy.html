2.4. Personal Data Information System: Databases and technologies used to process personal data.
2.5. Depersonalization: Actions that make it impossible to identify a user without additional information.

2.8. Personal Data: Any information relating directly or indirectly to a specific user of https://myapp.lv.
2.9. User: Any visitor to https://myapp.lv.
2.10. Provision of personal data: Actions aimed at disclosing personal data to specific persons.
2.11. Distribution of personal data: Actions aimed at making personal data available to an unlimited number of people, including publication online or in the media.
2.12. Cross-border transfer: Transfer of personal data to foreign countries or organizations.
2.13. Destruction of personal data: Actions that irreversibly destroy personal data so it cannot be restored.
